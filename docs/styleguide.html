Durations 80 · 130 · 200 · 320ms; standard ease cubic-bezier(.2,0,0,1). Every transform gated by reduced-motion.

Layers — depth encodes importance

Three material languages stack by z-depth. Pick a layer by an element's ROLE, never by taste. Most pixels are flat.

Layer 1 · Flat
Information at rest
Tables, text, surfaces. Fog canvas · white cards · hairlines. ~90% of the UI.
Layer 2 · Soft
Objects you work within
Dialogs, panels, widgets, recessed text wells. Neumorphic.
Layer 3 · Glass
The live layer
Button hover, CTAs, the ActionDock, priority alerts.

Brand font

Urbanist (OFL) — @fontsource/urbanist, weights 400/500/600/700. Loaded once in the app entry, mirrored in the kit theme.

The accent

Radiant Orange #FF4F18 — the single accent. Fills/CTAs carry white text; links use slate (#3B5568), never the accent.
